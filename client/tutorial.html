Chess Royale Tutorial

Welcome to Chess Royale! This tutorial will help you learn about the special pieces and features that make this game unique. Read about the Fantasy Pieces abilities and skills down below

How to play: Click on a white piece to select it, then click on a highlighted square to move. You can see each piece's abilities by hovering over it.

Special Pieces

Pegasus

The Pegasus moves like a Knight but can jump over obstacles like walls. This makes it excellent for navigating through difficult terrain.

Gorgon

The Gorgon has limited movement (2 squares diagonally or orthogonally), but when it captures a piece, all adjacent pieces are stunned for 2 turns!

Chimera (Lion and Goat)

The Chimera Lion combines the power of the Queen with some Knight-like moves, making it an extremely versatile attacker.

Juggernaut

The Lion moves like a Knight, while the Goat moves like a Bishop (limited to 2 squares). They must alternate turns and can move independently but stay within 5-6 tiles. When fully stretched with up to two enemy pieces between them, they snap back, capturing those pieces. If one half is captured, so is the other.

Builder

The Builder can create and remove walls. First move the Builder, then click on an empty square to place a wall or click on an existing wall to remove it.

Obstacles

Walls: Walls block movement for most pieces. Only the Pegasus can jump over them, and the Juggernaut can break through them.

Mud: When a piece lands on mud, it must stop its movement. On the next turn, it can move normally. The Juggernaut can move through mud without stopping.
